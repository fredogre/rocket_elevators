Overhead Protection
An angled deck protects crews from overhead hazards.
Working Deck
Used by Rocket Elevator crews to install additional elevator rails, allowing the Rocket system to climb as construction progresses.
Machine System Platforms
Central to the system’s unique design, the platforms support all machines, controllers, drives and travelling cables – components that become part of the permanent Rocket elevator.
Hydraulic Platform
The heart of the Rocket Elevator system, the hydraulic piston moves the whole assembly upward in single-floor jumps.
Retractable Support Beams
The Rocket Elevator system is held in place by support beams that are retracted during the climbing operation.
